## What is MiroFish?

MiroFish is a next-generation AI prediction engine powered by multi-agent technology. By extracting seed information from the real world—such as breaking news, policy drafts, or financial signals—it automatically constructs a high-fidelity parallel digital world.

Within this digital environment, thousands of intelligent agents with independent personalities, long-term memory, and behavioral logic freely interact and undergo social evolution. You can inject variables dynamically from a "God's-eye view" to precisely deduce future trajectories.

**Rehearse the future in a digital sandbox, and win decisions after countless simulations.**

## Use Cases

MiroFish bridges macro decision-making with micro creativity:

**For Decision Makers**
- Test policies and public relations strategies at zero risk
- Predict market reactions to financial decisions
- Analyze social trends and public opinion evolution
- Simulate crisis response scenarios

**For Creators**
- Explore alternate story endings and plot developments
- Generate creative scenarios based on world-building
- Simulate character interactions and relationships
- Predict audience reactions to creative choices

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Seed Materials">
    Provide data analysis reports, news articles, stories, or any text/PDF materials that seed the simulation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Graph Construction">
    MiroFish extracts entities, relationships, and context to build a knowledge graph using GraphRAG
  </Step>
  <Step title="Environment Setup">
    AI agents are generated with unique personas based on the entities in your seed materials
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Simulation">
    Agents interact freely in a dual-platform parallel simulation environment, evolving over time
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Reports">
    Specialized Report Agents analyze the simulation results and generate comprehensive prediction reports
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interact & Explore">
    Chat with any agent in the simulation or query the Report Agent for deeper insights
  </Step>
</Steps>
